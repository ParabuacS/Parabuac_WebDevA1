Chicken Giniling

Chicken Giniling is an improvised version of the Philippine dish Pork Giniling, where minced chicken is used instead of minced pork.
The method, ingredients, and taste, are mostly the same, save for the meat used.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb ground chicken
  • 1 1/2 cups potatoes diced
  • 1 cup carrots diced
  • 8 ounces tomato sauce
  • 6 cloves garlic crushed
  • 1 onion minced

  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 boiled eggs

Method

  1. Preparations
    • Heat a cooking pot and pour-in the cooking oil.
  2. Cooking
    1. When the oil is hot enough, put-in the garlic and sauté until the color turns light brown.
    2. Add the onions and sauté until the texture becomes soft.
    3. Put-in the ground pork and cook for 5 minutes.
    4. Add the beef or pork cube, tomato sauce, and water and let boil. Simmer for 20 minutes.
    5. Put the carrots and potatoes in then stir until every ingredient is properly distributed. Simmer for 10 to 12 minutes.
    6. Add salt, ground black pepper, and sugar then stir.
  3. Service
    1. Put in the boiled eggs and turn off the heat.
    2. Transfer to a serving bowl and serve.
    3. Share and enjoy!
